To the one I used to call my princess, my cinta hati

Maybe one day, bila I dah jadi the best version of myself, I’ll find my way back to you.

I never thought I’d have to write this, because honestly, I still love you., dan you akan sentiasa jadi seseorang yang penting dalam hidup I.

You were more than just someone I loved. You were my home, my peace, my comfort. You nampak semua sisi I : yang baik, yang teruk, yang hilang arah, ada masa I tak layak pun untuk disayangi. You percaya dekat I masa I sendiri pun tak yakin dengan diri sendiri.

You were there through everything the hard times, the late nights, the moments I wanted to give up. Setiap kejayaan yang I capai hari ni, ada bahagian dari you dalam tu. Sebab you pernah jadi tulang belakang I, sumber kekuatan I.

I tahu you tunggu I lama. You gave me time, love, patience and endless understanding. Tapi masa tu I belum ready. I was lost in my own mind, full of things I didn’t know how to fix. I was scared. Scared that if I came back too soon, I’d hurt you again. And I never wanted to see you in pain because of me.

Sekarang I tahu you dah cuba buka hati untuk orang lain, and I faham. You deserve that. You deserve someone yang cintakan you dengan penuh keyakinan, who gives you peace, who never makes you doubt yourself.

I nampak cara dia layan you, dan honestly, dia baik untuk you. She loves you softly, calmly, the way I never managed to. And I’m happy for you. You finally have the love you always deserved.

I chose to undur diri buat sementara. Not because I stopped loving you, but because I love you enough to let you breathe, to let you find peace even if that peace no longer includes me.

I minta maaf untuk semua sakit yang I pernah bagi. Untuk setiap kali I buat you menangis, setiap masa I buat you rasa tak cukup, setiap kali I gagal tunjukkan kasih dengan cara yang betul. You tak pernah deserve semua tu. You deserve the world, cinta yang terbaik, dan hati yang tak keliru.

You’re just too perfect, Yana. Thank you sebab you selalu masak untuk I, sebab you tahu I kadang tak sempat makan atau tengah stress dekat office. I still remember bila you tiba-tiba hantar makanan yang you masak and I swear, that moment I rasa tenang. Makan masakan you buat I rasa macam you ada dengan I.

Dulu masa I demam, you masakkan sup ayam untuk I. I suka sangat sup ayam you, sampai sekarang I masih ingat rasa dia. Bila I warded dulu, you datang jaga I tanpa banyak soal. You selalu ada, even bila I tak minta. And now that all of that is gone, I realize how much those moments meant to me. I lost something I can never replace.

Yana, please know that you sangat-sangat bernilai dekat dunia ni. You’re kind, thoughtful, and full of love. And anyone who gets to have you in their life is really lucky. I just hope you never forget how special you are, and how much you’ve done for people, especially me.

Kadang I terfikir macam mana kalau masa tu boleh untuk kita putar, if I had been ready. If I had known how to love without fear, tapi mungkin semua ni ada sebab. Mungkin I kena kehilangan you untuk belajar apa makna sebenar cinta.

I still want you, but maybe not now. Maybe one day, when I’ve healed completely, when I can love without fear, when I can stay without running. And if by that time our paths ever cross again, I’ll hold your hand differently: dengan tenang, dengan penuh cinta, dengan segala yang I tak sempat bagi dulu.

Tapi kalau hari tu tak pernah datang pun, it's okay. Because you’ll always be a part of me. You’ll always be my lesson, my reason, my once-in-a-lifetime.

So this is my goodbye. Not because I stopped loving you, tapi sebab I dah belajar yang cinta sebenar kadang-kadang bermaksud melepaskan. Melepaskan dengan membenarkan you mencari your own happiness, kebahagiaan yang mungkin you tak sempat rasa masa dengan I dulu.

And if one day you ever need me, even just as a friend, you can always reach me. Sebab apa pun jadi, you pernah jadi tulang belakang I, cinta hati I, dan kawan terbaik I.

Kalau masa boleh diulang, I’d still choose you every single time. Tapi sekarang I pilih untuk sembuh dan perbaiki diri I, dan biar you bahagia, walaupun bukan dengan I. Sebab I percaya satu hari nanti, you akan betul-betul bahagia. Dan itu pun dah cukup untuk I.

— Hans, once yours.
